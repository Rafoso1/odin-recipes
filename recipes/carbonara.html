Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Try this recipe if you want to make an authentic, creamy carbonara that comes straight from Italy, where I live. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • guanciale (cured pork)
  • eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • spaghetti pasta
  • lots of black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Chop the guanciale into small pieces and fry it in a pan
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add the cheese and pepper
  4. Cook the pasta in the boiling water
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the guanciale
  6. Add the egg mixture and stir until the sauce is creamy
