Pizza Margarita

Try this classic Italian pizza recipe that features fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone
  4. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese
  5. Arrange the tomato slices and mozzarella cheese on top of the dough
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza
  7. Season with salt and pepper
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted
  9. Remove from the oven and sprinkle fresh basil leaves on top
  10. Slice and serve hot
